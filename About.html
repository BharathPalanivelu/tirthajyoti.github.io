About

Work

Currently, I work as a Senior Principal Engineer at ON Semiconductor in the MOSFET Device Design group. I am also leading AI/ML based projects to add value to semiconductor design and product innovations. My projects involve interesting ideas such as...

  • neural network-in-circuit-simulation loop,
  • deep learning based design quality recommendation,
  • edge AI in power management,

    • Data science, Machine learning

    Apart from my work, I do personal projects on data science and machine learning. I am a data science communicator, publishing regularly on popular online platforms such as Medium, AnalyticsVidya, Opensource.com, and KDnuggets.

    My first data science related book "Data wrangling with Python" was published on February, 2019. Currently, I am working on a second book about hands-on mathematics for data scientists.

    Formal education

    I have a Ph.D. in EE from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Chicago and a B.Tech in Instrumentation Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kharagpur.

    I am also pursuing an Masters of Science (MS) Degree in Analytics (specializing in Computational Data Analytics) from Georgia Tech, starting Fall 2018.

    Contribution to the technical community

    In 2015, I was elevated to the grade of Senior Member of IEEE for my contributions towards power electronics. I have authored/co-authored more than 25 peer-reviewed Transaction and Conference papers, 2 monographs/book chapters, and 4 U.S. Patents. I also serve on the technical program committee as Track/Topic chair in numerous IEEE conferences.
